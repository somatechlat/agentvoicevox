# AgentVoiceBox: The Open-Source Voice AI Platform

## What Is AgentVoiceBox?

AgentVoiceBox is a **100% open-source, voice-powered AI assistant platform** that lets businesses deploy intelligent voice agents capable of having natural, real-time conversations with customers. Think of it as giving your business a team of tireless, multilingual voice assistants that can answer calls, help customers, and complete tasks—24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

**Completely free. Fully transparent. Community-driven.**

Unlike proprietary solutions that lock you into expensive subscriptions and hide their inner workings, AgentVoiceBox gives you the complete source code. You can inspect it, modify it, extend it, and deploy it however you want—forever, at no cost.

Unlike traditional phone systems that force callers through frustrating menu trees ("Press 1 for sales, Press 2 for support..."), AgentVoiceBox creates **conversational experiences** where customers simply speak naturally and get immediate, intelligent responses.
